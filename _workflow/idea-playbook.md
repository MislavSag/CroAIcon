# Idea playbook

What makes a CroAIcon post idea worth building. The brainstorm skill and the idea agents score every candidate against this. An idea that fails the bar goes to the backlog as parked, not to a draft.

## A good idea clears five tests

1. **The data answers it.** The columns and tables we trust can produce the finding. Today that means `db_afs`, with `employeecounteop` and `nacerev21`. If it needs financial columns, it waits.
2. **There is a real finding.** A number that moves, a gap that surprises, a reversal. Not a flat line dressed up as a story. Name the expected magnitude. Small and dull fails.
3. **The angle is clear.** You can state the [KUT] in one sentence. A finding with no human angle is a chart, not a post.
4. **It is new.** Check `_workflow/ideas-backlog.md`. We have not published it or already parked it as done.
5. **It is reproducible in a sitting.** One or two scripts and a handful of charts. If it sprawls into a research project, split it or shelve it.

## Shape of a logged idea

- **Hook.** A two beat headline in the house voice.
- **Finding.** The one sentence result, with the rough magnitude.
- **Data.** The table and columns it needs, and whether they are trusted.
- **Angle.** The [KUT] in one line.
- **Effort.** Small, medium, or large.
- **Verdict.** Build now, park, or drop, with one line of why.
